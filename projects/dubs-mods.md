**Stack**: C#, Rimworld modding API

## dubs-heatpump
Adds realistic heat pump systems to Rimworld. Automatic heating/cooling mode switching based on temperature logic. Separate duct networks. No known issues.

## dubs-electrolysis
Electrolysis system mod for Rimworld.

## bad-hygiene-plus
Extension/improvement mod for the popular Dubs Bad Hygiene mod.
