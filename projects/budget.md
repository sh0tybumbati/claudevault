# Budget

**GitHub**: https://github.com/sh0tybumbati/budget-tracker
**Local**: ~/projects/Budget/
**Stack**: React 19, Node.js, Express, SQLite, Tailwind CSS, Vite, Docker
**State**: 80–85% complete. Feature-complete, Docker-ready. Last commit Jun 2025 (stale).

## What it is
Modern personal finance tracker. Real-time budget monitoring, categorized entries, interactive charts, timeline and calendar views, dark/light mode, responsive design. Docker-ready with automatic sync.

## Commercial potential
MEDIUM-HIGH — crowded market (YNAB, EveryDollar) but self-hosting angle is a differentiator for privacy-conscious users. Could monetize as SaaS with subscription tiers, or sell as a one-time self-hosted download.

## Status
Feature-complete but not actively maintained or monetized. Not in current income plan.
