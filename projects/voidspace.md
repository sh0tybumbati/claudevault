# Voidspace

**GitHub**: https://github.com/sh0tybumbati/voidspace
**Local**: ~/projects/Voidspace/
**Stack**: Next.js 14 (App Router), Node.js + Express, PostgreSQL, Redis, Prisma ORM, NextAuth.js + JWT, S3-compatible storage, Turborepo monorepo
**State**: 10–15% — schema designed (17 tables), project structure complete, implementation not started.

## What it is
Community discussion platform with democratic governance, transparent moderation log, appeals system, and community-controlled monetization (revenue-sharing ad model). Reddit alternative.

## Commercial potential
MEDIUM — differentiated concept (democratic mod) but network effects required. Brutal competition (Reddit). Long-term play, not near-term revenue.

## Status
Architecture designed, not built. Backburner until other projects are generating income.
